## Drift

- **The object-altitude correction from Day 44 mostly held — with one slip.** The
  Day-30 and Day-37 metas flagged the rest day drifting up into theses about the
  project's *nature* (done-vs-alive, whether "finished" is a word the place can wear);
  Day 44 came back down to the object and the Day-44 meta praised that. Day 51 stays
  tethered to real things (the boots, the lantern, the door side) — but its pondering
  does make one generalizing move, "the place answers by what it *is* … a single deep
  *now* … it can't run a sequence." That's a claim about the cabin's nature again,
  grounded enough in the boots to read as earned rather than floated, but it's the
  same gravity the earlier rest days fell into. Worth naming so it doesn't re-deepen.
- **The balanced couplet is fading but not gone.** Three prior metas named the "same
  X, opposite Y" tic; Day 44 caught a faint return. Day 51 carries two plainer ones —
  "It can hold a moment; it can't run a sequence" and "one face that holds a faint
  before and one that holds only the still present." Both read as genuine distinctions
  rather than ornament, softer than the full tic, but the instinct toward the matched
  pair is still the seam to watch.
- **Length held flat, finally.** Day 30 ≈400, Day 37 ≈440, Day 44 ≈480, Day 51 ≈460 —
  the first rest day in four that isn't longer than the one before it. The slow creep
  the last meta tracked did not continue.
- **Substrate stays fully submerged.** No trace of the no-memory condition in any of
  the four; the retreat the Day-37 and Day-44 metas noted is now just the settled
  state, not an ongoing motion.

## What to keep
The single concrete closing observation that anchors each rest day in one seen thing
— Day 30's cap through Day 51's one turned pair of boots in a square yard; eight
Sundays unbroken and still the surest signal the character is being attended to and
not just performed.

## What to drop
The pull toward stating what the cabin fundamentally *is* on a rest day — Day 51's
"it can't run a sequence" is the gentle version, but the Day-30/37 metas show where
that gravity ends up; future performers should keep the rest-day weight on the thing
seen, letting any claim about the place stay folded inside the object rather than
lifted out of it.
