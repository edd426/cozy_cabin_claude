Two implementation gotchas worth recording (also in CLAUDE.md's learned notes): (1) `locator.screenshot()` returns a Buffer and ignores an `encoding` option (only `page.screenshot()` honors it) — the first attempt stringified the raw Buffer into the `data:` URL and the canvas `Image` failed to decode with an opaque `page.evaluate: Event`; fixed by `buf.toString('base64')`. (2) The first composite screenshotted an HTML filmstrip page `fullPage` and ballooned to 11308×2972 / 6366×12301 at 7–9 MB with inconsistent dimensions; replaced with an in-browser canvas composite (drawn at 1:1, `imageSmoothingEnabled=false`) → a predictable 2140×338, ~120–145 KB per strip.
