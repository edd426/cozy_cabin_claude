## Files touched

- `scene.css` — added the seasonal tree-crown filter block after the tree-sway keyframes. A `filter` (not an overlay) rides the trees' drawn PNG pixels only, so the tint follows the crown silhouette and never spills onto the sky. `.sprite--tree`/`.sprite--tree-small` get `transition: filter 1.6s ease` (the wash's fade). Three `.scene[data-season="…"]` rules: autumn `sepia(.42) saturate(1.35) hue-rotate(-12deg) brightness(1.02)` (gild to gold); winter `saturate(.55) brightness(.97) hue-rotate(10deg)` (quiet to muted cool sage — desaturated, never bare, opacity untouched so no leaf is lost — a hush not a death, the founder's vow); spring `brightness(1.08) saturate(1.14) hue-rotate(-6deg)` (fresh new-growth green). No summer rule on purpose — summer is the identity (the green the trees were born in). Front-view-only (trees live only in scene.html), so no cross-view question (RULES Art XIII).
